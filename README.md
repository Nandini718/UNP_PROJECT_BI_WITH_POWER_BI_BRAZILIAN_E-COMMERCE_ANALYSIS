# UNP_PROJECT_BI_WITH_POWER_BI_BRAZILIAN_E-COMMERCE_ANALYSIS

I did this project in collaboration with UNP, where I developed a comprehensive and interactive Business Intelligence dashboard using Power BI based on a Brazilian E-commerce dataset. The main objective of the project was to analyze and visualize key business metrics such as sales revenue, customer distribution, payment behavior, order performance, seller activity, and customer satisfaction.
In this project, I worked with raw data and performed data cleaning, transformation, and modeling using Power Query and DAX to ensure accurate analysis. I designed multiple dashboards including Revenue Analysis, Customer Analysis, Payment Analysis, Seller Analysis, Order Analysis, and Review Analysis, each providing detailed insights into different aspects of the business.
The dashboards included dynamic filters and slicers that allowed users to explore data by region, time period, product category, and payment type. Through this analysis, I identified important trends such as high usage of credit card payments, regional variations in customer and seller distribution, fluctuations in monthly revenue, and the impact of order delivery on customer satisfaction.
Based on the insights, I also provided practical business recommendations such as improving marketing strategies in low-performing regions, reducing order cancellations, promoting underperforming products, and enhancing customer experience in areas with lower review scores.
Overall, this project enhanced my skills in data visualization, data analysis, and dashboard design using Power BI, and demonstrated how data-driven insights can support better decision-making and improve business performance.
